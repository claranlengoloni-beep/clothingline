Our Heritage

Aura & Thread Collective began as Thrift Thrills, a passion project dedicated to the hunt for hidden gems and the belief that quality should be accessible. We spent years identifying the superior stitch-work of vintage garments and the enduring feel of natural fibers.

As we curated the past, we began to envision the future. We took the lessons of durability and timeless style from our "thrift" roots and infused them into a new standard of professional wear.
